ॐ क्रीं कालिकायै नमः

Who is she?

The more I get to know her,
the more I realize I don't know anything.

How would you describe her?

She is everything I can think of;
also everything my brain is not able to comprehend.

How should someone start worshipping her?

Worshipping her is not something you start;
it is her who decides if you are capable of.

What can a person offer to the goddess?

Nothing except pure love from our hearts.
