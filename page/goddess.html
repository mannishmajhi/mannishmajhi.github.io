The word "काल" has two meanings viz. time and death.
  • Time brings death to everything.
  • The three eyes in goddesses' forehead represent the three directions of time; the past, the present and the future.
  • Her dark complexion represents the color of space. It symbolizes that universe came from nothingness and will return back to nothingness.
  • Her ten faces represent ten great wisdoms (दस माहाविद्या); starting from Kali and ending in Kamala.
  • She is naked. It means she herself is the universe; everything is within her and nothing is outside so that it may cover her.
  • Her skirt is made up of severed hands. It symbolizes that goddess is above all kinds of karma.
  • Her hair is not done. It symbolizes that even though if goddess loves us like our mother, she is free to act on her own.
  • She wears the garland of skulls. It symbolizes that alphabets of Sanskrit language originated from her.
  • There is a severed head of a demon in one of her hand. It symbolizes that goddess can be perceived only after one's ego is slain.
  • She is always portrayed with Shiva. It symbolizes that Shakti and Shiva are incomplete without each other.
