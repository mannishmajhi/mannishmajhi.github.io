The word "काल" has three meanings viz. time, death and black color.
  • Time brings death to everything.
  • The three eyes in goddesses' forehead represent the three directions of time; the past, the present and the future.
  • Her dark complexion represents the color of emptiness. It symbolizes that our universe just came into being from her desire and will return back to nothingness with her desire.
She has ten heads, hands and legs.

Her ten faces represent ten great wisdoms (दस माहाविद्या); starting from Kali and ending in Kamala.

She is naked.

The lack of clothing symbolizes that nothing can contain nature as she herself is the entirety.

There are severed hands on her hip.

It symbolizes that nature is above any and all kind of कर्म; she can act as she desires.

Her hair is not done.

It symbolizes her freedom. Even though she loves her children, she is not bound by the motherly duties imposed by our societies.

There is a severed head of a demon in one of her hand.

It symbolizes that goddess can be perceived only after one's ego has been slain.

She is wielding different kinds of weapon ih her hands.

It symbolizes that nature is the source of all knowledge and different kind of education is used to slay different kind of demon present in us.

She wears the garland of skulls.

The skulls symbolize that alphabets of language. So, every language originated from her and she adores the children who have love for knowledge; as it a path to enlightment.

She is always portrayed with Shiva.

It symbolizes that Shakti and Shiva are incomplete without each other.
